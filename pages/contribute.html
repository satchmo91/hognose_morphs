This site is entirely made possible by donated images. If you would like to contribute to the site, instructions are below.

IMPORTANT!

Please only submit images if you...
  • Have rights to the images
  • Consent to me using your photos for this site or any future project
  • Are at least reasonably certain of the genetics of the animal in the photo.


To submit images, please email Erik at hognosegenes@gmail.com with your images attached. The email should state the genes displayed in the photo, and the name of the person or business that should be credited for the image.
